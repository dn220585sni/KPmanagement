Investments

Our investment focus includes the following areas:

  • Pharmaceutical operations and related services;
  • Medical practices and healthcare providers;
  • Healthcare technologies and supporting infrastructure.

The Company holds investment interests across selected European jurisdictions.

We place particular emphasis on healthcare technologies and projects involving intellectual property, brands and other intangible assets.

Each opportunity is assessed through market analysis and legal review. Our approach is practical, commercially focused and based on sector expertise.

We actively manage our investment portfolio and continue to explore new opportunities within the European healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors.

We maintain transparent and constructive relationships with the companies in which we invest.

Activities

We are a UK-based investment and advisory company holding investments across Europe, primarily in the healthcare, pharmacy and medical practice sectors.

Our activities combine investment operations, holding company functions, information services and management consultancy. We support businesses through equity participation, structured investment solutions and the management of intellectual property assets.

The Company operates in accordance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements and follows sound corporate governance principles.

The Company does not carry out pharmaceutical or medical activities and does not provide regulated financial, medical or pharmaceutical services.
